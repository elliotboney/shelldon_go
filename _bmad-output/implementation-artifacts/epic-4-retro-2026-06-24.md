## Carried-forward technical debt

**Architectural — track, and the first two get higher-stakes in Epic 5:**
- **Non-atomic `PromoteLearning` + `AppendFact` (4.5)** — partial failure leaves a learning `promoted` in sqlite but its observation absent from markdown. True atomicity needs a write-ahead journal or compensating undo.
- **Race: dream `PromoteLearning` vs concurrent `ApplyLearning` UPSERT (4.5)** — capture's UPSERT resets `status='pending'`, could silently undo a promotion. Mitigated by WAL + AD-6 single-writer today.
- **In-memory budget/cooldown (4.5 / 3.6 AI-4)** — resets on restart; crash-loop could exceed `dreamBudgetPerDay`.
- **`ApplyMemoryOps` silently drops promote/prune ops** if ever routed through dispatch — no structural guard (nothing routes them there now).

**Trivial / acceptable at single-user M2:** recurrence filter applied post-SQL not in query (candidates past row 20 invisible); `extractJSONArray` ignores string literals (graceful no-op); duplicate-JSON-key last-write-wins; `AppendFact` accepts arbitrary relPath beyond `WriteFile`'s vault reject; empty-`convoID` orphans; last-turn-on-shutdown drop; `convo_id` not in FTS table (full scan, fine at volume).
